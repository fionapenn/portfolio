ABOUT

I am an artist currently living in Richmond, VA.
I am working towards a BFA in Kinetic Imaging and a minor in Anthropology from VCUarts.
I explore themes of self identification and family, often through the lens of anthropology and linguistics.
I work at the intersection of 3d animation, appropriated video, and node-based programming.
I also have an interest in music and the way it can be tied to community building.

I also love to code in many forms (including this website!).
